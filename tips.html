Do’s and Don’ts

  • ✅ Learn basic French greetings.
  • ✅ Try local foods in small cafes.
  • ❌ Don’t be loud in public places.
  • ❌ Avoid tipping excessively (service is usually included).

Travel Tips

Use trains for city-to-city travel, carry euros, and always keep your passport safe. Book museum tickets online to skip queues.
